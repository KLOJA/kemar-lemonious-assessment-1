Our History

The 5K running competition is a yearly contest that pushes participants to the limits as they run across a very challeneging 5k trail. It covers a 1 kilometre elevation and drop. A Two (2) kilometre Beach section and a 2 Kilometre asphalt section. The proceeds of the race are donated to a different charity voted on by our patreons before the event.
